Craigslist Redesign

UI

INTRODUCTION

As one of the most popular websites for classified ads, Craiglist gains a lot of traffic everyday. As a service, the idea is brilliant and that is why it's a huge success today. While the UX works fine, the design itself is somewhat controversial because it has only had minor updates in the past 15 years.

CONTEXT

There are multiple conflicting thoughts concerning the design for Craigslist. Some might say the sites design is the reason for it's functional UX. Others believe the reason why Craigslist offers a positive user experience is simply because it has not changed in so long and people are comfortable with using it. I wanted to give the site a modern look without complicating the design. Making it clear and easy for all types of people to use. This is my design process for a modern Craigslist.

PROCESS & SOLUTION

I wanted to keep the staple Craigslist purple but add a bit more color to create a strong brand color palette. I added clean simple graphics to sort through categories. Craigslist's current design is comprised of lists and lists of cities, items, job postings etc. I ditched the lists and used dropdowns to save space and easily navigate through fields.
